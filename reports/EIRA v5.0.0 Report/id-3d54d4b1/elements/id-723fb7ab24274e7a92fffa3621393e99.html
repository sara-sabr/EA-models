Digital Agenda ()
DEFINITION: Digital Agenda ABB is a Goal focusing on better access for consumers and businesses to digital goods and services across Europe by providing the EU with an advanced system of user rights and protection for consumers and businesses. Source: Europarl (https://www.europarl.europa.eu/factsheets/en/sheet/64/digital-agenda-for-europe)
