Business Analytics Service ()
Shares the functionalities of i) building analysis models and simulations to create scenarios, ii) understand realities and/or iii) predict future states. Business analytics includes data mining, predictive analytics, applied analytics and statistics, and is delivered as an application suitable for a business user. These analytics solutions often come with prebuilt industry content that is targeted at an industry business process (for example, claims, underwriting or a specific regulatory requirement).
