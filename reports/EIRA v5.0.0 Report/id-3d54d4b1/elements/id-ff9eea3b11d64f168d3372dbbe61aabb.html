Public Policy Objective ()
DEFINITION: Public Policy Objective ABB is a Goal allowing public authorities to bring social change in the medium and long term, in a given area. It is based on certain values and objectives and is implemented using a variety of methods. It applies on the territory within which the authority is authorised to act.
