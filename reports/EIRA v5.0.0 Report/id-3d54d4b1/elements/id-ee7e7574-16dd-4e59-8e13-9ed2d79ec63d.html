Once Only ()
DEFINITION: Once Only ABB is a Principle entailing that citizens and businesses provide diverse data only once in contact with public administrations, while public administration bodies take actions to internally share and reuse these data – even across borders – always in respect of data protection regulations and other constraints. Source: Europa (https://ec.europa.eu/digital-building-blocks/wikis/display/CEFDIGITAL/2018/10/25/TOOP:+The+Once-Only+Principle#:~:text=On%201%20January%202017%2C%20the%20European%20Commission%20launched,diverse%20data%20only%20once%20to%20a%20public%20administration.)
