Customer & Loyalty Data Diagram ()
This describes the relation of information objects and entities within the Marketing & Customer Care Capability (Domain). All marketing-related information objects and entities are related to either the Customer or Passenger entity. Certain KPIs and data is collected from the passenger; i.e., loyalty data, feedback, or complaints. This data is used to create a loyalty data profile and helps to develop personalized marketing campaigns which are used to attract more customers.
