Cargo Activity Diagram ()
The business process of the Cargo Capability (Domain) is separated in two main pools: airline and forwarder. The process starts with Receive Shipment from Shipper. The operations are responsible for enabling the airline to receive the shipment. All warehouse activities and freight preparation for the flight need to be done before departure. The departure activities send the flight manifest to the next station. After executing the arrival activities, all cargo is shipped to the forwarder. Finally, all operating cargo arrives at the destination.
