Revenue Accounting Management ()
Capability to account for revenue (earned and forward sales revenue) accounting activities including: - customer invoicing (the process of developing and delivering customer invoices including creating methods and procedures for communicating and delivering invoices and statements for services and products, the maintenance of customer/product master files, the generation and transmittal of customer billing data to the customer, the posting of receivable entries, and the resolution of customer billing inquiries.) - calculation of ticket taxes, fares and commission - accounting for refunds/exchanges - charge backs - mail revenue - cargo revenue - backend deal payments - auditing and reporting of all the above.
