Forwarding ()
Forwarding is part of the Cargo Capability (Domain). According to (IATA, Air Cargo Industry Master Operation Plan) some cargo operations are regarded to the forwarder. The following tasks are within the scope of this sub domain. It receive shipment validates security and customs status of a shipment, unloads the trucks, screening shipment, places freight into a warehouse. Moreover it is responsible for the handover shipment to forwarder includes also verifying customs release status, handover documents and preparing shipment for handover.
