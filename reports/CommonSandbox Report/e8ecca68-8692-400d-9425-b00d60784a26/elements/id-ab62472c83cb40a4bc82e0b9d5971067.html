Deliver Greater Privacy & Transparency In WHO Accesses WHAT Data, WHY, and Using Which Tools ()
More critical, success of this proposal depends on managing privacy effectively. A current barrier to data access, and thereby to value of data, are security controls designed to assure privacy that indiscriminately block lawful, ethical, appropriate use. This proposal delivers greater privacy and greater transparency in who is accessing what data, for what purpose, and with what tools. It assures ethical, authorized access that is faster, simpler, more reliable and less costly.
