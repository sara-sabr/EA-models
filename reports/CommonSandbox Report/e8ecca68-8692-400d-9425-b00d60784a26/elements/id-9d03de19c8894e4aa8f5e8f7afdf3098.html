Design For Performance, Availability & Scalability ()
Design for performance, availability and scalability - ensure response times meet user needs, and critical services are highly available - support zero‑downtime deployments for planned and unplanned maintenance - use distributed architectures, assume failure will happen, handle errors gracefully, and monitor performance and behaviour actively - establish architectures that supports new technology insertion with minimal disruption to existing programs and services - control technical diversity; design systems based on modern technologies and platforms already in use
