Risk - Interdependencies With Partners (Assessment) ()
Risk Description: Due to the collaborative nature of the work that is to be done within the scope of the WLM project, there is a risk that the partner processes outside of IITB may impact the progress of the work being done. These partners currently include SSC. A delay in the process of any of these partners could potentially affect the WLM Project timelines and cause delays and unexpected financial costs. Impact Description: There is a risk that the timeline is affected, also impacting the project costs. The scope might also need to be revised if partners cannot proceed with the required activities.
