Growing Need to Obtain & Integrate Data From Beyond ESDC ()
In addition, there is a growing need to obtain and integrate data from beyond ESDC. Efforts to better understand our clients and support vulnerable, racialized and marginalized groups can only go so far without bringing in critical pieces of data from key players, including provinces and territories, municipalities and third-party organizations. Issues such as chronic homelessness need national support, but the data to understand such issues is scattered across stakeholders.
