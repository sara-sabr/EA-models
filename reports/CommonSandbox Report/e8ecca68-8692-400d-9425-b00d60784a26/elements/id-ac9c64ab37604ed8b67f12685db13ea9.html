8. Integrity & Quality-by-Design ()
BDM Architecture Principle 8: Principle: Integrity & Quality-by-Design. Statement: BDM will embed integrity and quality controls up-front and throughout processes and services. Rationale: Embedded approach to integrity and quality management is required to move away from “pay-and-chase” model and enable continuous service improvement. Implications: • BDM will ease burden of proof on clients, based on risk and complexity profile, while maintaining compliance and integrity checks. • Client risk, priority and complexity profile will result from integrity and quality controls embedded in processes and services. • Quality and continuous improvement will be embedded in all processes.
