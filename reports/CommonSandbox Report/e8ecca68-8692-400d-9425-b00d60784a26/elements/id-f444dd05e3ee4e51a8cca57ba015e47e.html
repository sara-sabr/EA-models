Connecting to Benefits Delivery Modernization Milestones Benefits Delivery Modernization (BDM) is a large-scale transformation of the Employment Insurance (EI), Canada Pension Plan (CPP), and Old Age Security (OAS) programs and services, designed to deliver an improved client service delivery experience. Did you know that one of Enterprise Architecture’s main priorities for the next three years is the alignment and realization of the BDM programme? As Enterprise Architecture (EA) practitioners, we will be able to claim success when architectural investments: • contribute to or accelerate the realization of the target state of BDM outcomes; and • align to the BDM target state.
