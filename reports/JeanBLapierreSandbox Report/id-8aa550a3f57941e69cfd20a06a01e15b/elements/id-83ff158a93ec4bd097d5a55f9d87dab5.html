Increased Account Lock-Outs (Assessment) ()
• At least 30,000 NSLSC accounts are now locked out, and that number increases each day. The causes for this include: o GCKey username and password credentials are being forgotten by clients, thus preventing successful NSLSC login and contributing to the increasing tally of account lockouts. o NSLSC security questions and answers are being forgotten by clients, thus preventing successful credential substitution, and sustaining the account lockout tally.
