Service Delivery Requires IT Security ()
Service delivery requires IT security. IT security is an integral part of continuous program and service delivery. To avoid the loss of service and trust that IT security breaches can cause, departments need to view IT security as a business imperative; a "service enabler." Although program and service delivery managers may delegate responsibility for IT security to technical experts, they remain accountable to the Deputy Head and are responsible for ensuring the security of the programs and services under their authority.
