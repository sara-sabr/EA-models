11 - Incorporate Digital Principles Into Operations, Programs & Service Design ()
In order to guide and shape how government information, technology and service delivery will be managed in a new digital ecosystem, ESDC is undertaking the following initiatives:  CSLP – E-Service Re-Procurement;  Temporary Foreign Worker System and Webservice Enhancements 2.0; and,  CSLP - New Brunswick SIN Validation. These initiatives will help build a set of digital standards into policy, operations and program development. In response to the COVID-19 pandemic, ESDC created and rapidly deployed the e-service and eSIN applications. Currently, over 99% of EI applications are currently submitted through digital methods. Additionally, Pensions projects, such as CPP-SIS, are further advancing the availability of e-services for that client segment.
