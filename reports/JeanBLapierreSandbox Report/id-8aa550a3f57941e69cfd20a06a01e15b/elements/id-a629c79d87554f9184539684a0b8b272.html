Xenos Khan (Business Actor) ()
Xenos Khan, MBA AEA Ottawa Gatineau Chapter Director Programs Designate Xenos passed away suddenly in May 2021. He was a gifted and warm hearted man dedicated to his family, friends, and his colleagues at Gelder, Gingras and Associates where he was Vice President Consulting and at Employment and Social Development Canada (ESDC) where he was working on the Cloud Reference Architecture. He will be sorely missed.
