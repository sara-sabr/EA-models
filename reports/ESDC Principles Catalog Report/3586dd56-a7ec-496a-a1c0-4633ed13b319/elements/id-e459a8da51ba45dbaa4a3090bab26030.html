10. Data Driven Decision Making ()
Statement Decisions are based on accurate and comprehensive data from authoritative sources Rationale • Data-driven insights enhance strategic planning • Data-driven practices can proactively identify trends, opportunities and innovation • Enables optimizing processes, automation and resource allocation maximizing business value and enhancing security • Helps reduce risks, vulnerabilities and compliance gaps • Objective and evidence-based decision-making leads to fair, inclusive and transparent outcomes Implications • Change management may be required to promote a culture that values data-driven insights and data literacy • Additional infrastructure and technical skills may be required to ensure data compatibility, accessibility, consistency and integration • Data retention, residency and privacy policies must be carefully managed • Over reliance on data may lead to missed qualitative insights and human intuition • Automated data-driven decision making may require human intervention
