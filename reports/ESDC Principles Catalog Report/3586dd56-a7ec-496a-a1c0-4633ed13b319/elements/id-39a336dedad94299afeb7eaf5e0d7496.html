Principles Catalog ()
The Principles catalog captures principles of the Business and Architecture Principles that describe what a "good" solution or architecture should look like. Principles are used to evaluate and agree an outcome for architecture decision points. Principles are also used as a tool to assist in architectural governance of change initiatives https://pubs.opengroup.org/architecture/togaf9-doc/arch/chap31.html
