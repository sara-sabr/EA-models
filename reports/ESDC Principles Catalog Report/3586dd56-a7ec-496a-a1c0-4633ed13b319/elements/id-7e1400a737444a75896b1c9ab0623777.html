2. Human-Centric & Digitally Accessible ()
Statement Human-centric and digitally accessible approach to enhance everyone’s user experience Rationale • Increased adoption and satisfaction when human needs are considered • Designing with users reduces risk in implementation, operations, troubleshooting and remediation • Accessibility by design to ensure everyone has access and can benefit equally • Users with distinct needs, language preferences and abilities should be engaged from the outset to ensure fairness, inclusivity and unbiasedness • What is delivered will work for everyone Implications • It can be very difficult to get feedback from all users • Various users’ requirements may drive scope creep and prioritization • Continuous enhancements may be necessary to address discovered needs • Resistance to change may impact success • Inconsistent and non-standard data capture compliance impacts availability, accessibility and usability of information
