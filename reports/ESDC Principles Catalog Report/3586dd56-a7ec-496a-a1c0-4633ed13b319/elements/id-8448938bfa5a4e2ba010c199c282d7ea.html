6. Security and Privacy ()
Statement Security and privacy by design Rationale • Designing with security from the start reduces rework and risk exposure • Staying current with security best practices and controls improves response, cost and efficiency • Protecting sensitive information and ensuring compliance with regulations is crucial for maintaining trust and reputation as well as avoiding breaches • Holistic, pragmatic and scalable Security and privacy practices ensure integrity, reliability and availability Implications • Ongoing investment in security and privacy is required to keep up with evolving threats and compliance requirements • Security is everyone’s responsibility requiring training, knowledge and skills • A strain on the organization resulting from continuous patching, testing, auditing and maintenance • Strict security and privacy measures may limit the ability to rapidly innovate or adopt new technologies
