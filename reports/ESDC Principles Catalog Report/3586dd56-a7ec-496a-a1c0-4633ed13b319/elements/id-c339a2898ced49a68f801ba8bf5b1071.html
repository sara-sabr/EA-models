9. Smart Infrastructure ()
Statement Host workloads on the most appropriate location, platform and provider Rationale • Smart infrastructure provides diverse hosting capability options • Greatest business value is realized when workloads are deployed using the most suited service model possible • Selecting the most appropriate hosting options will unlock the most business value and cost efficiency • Smart infrastructure optimizes resource utilization, improves service delivery and provides a robust foundation for future growth and innovation • Predictability, scalability, sensitivity, latency and simplicity are all core values that can be maximized by leveraging the inherent benefits from a hosting services provider Implications • High initial costs and risks with establishing and migrating to new hosting infrastructure • Cumulative security risks of managing and integrating multiple hosting platforms • Maintaining multiple platforms can drive unnecessary complexity, increase governance and require diverse skill set specialization • Ongoing training, skills and knowledge may drive change management and cost
