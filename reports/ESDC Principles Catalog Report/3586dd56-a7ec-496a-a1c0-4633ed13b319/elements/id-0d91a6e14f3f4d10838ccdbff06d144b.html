1. Business-Driven Architecture ()
Statement Architecture must be aligned with and driven by business goals and objectives Rationale • Alignment of IT investment with business priorities for better resource utilization • Deliver value by supporting business strategies while maintaining relevance • Ensuring business reasons behind any changes enables return on investment and value • Consideration given to future business needs and trends enables rapid adaptation Implications • Collaboration to achieve business goals through adaptive planning requires investment • Transparent and collaborative cross-functional teams to facilitate business alignment • Overemphasis on short-term goals may diminish long-term strategic planning • Continuous alignment with dynamic business needs may require frequent changes to processes and systems
