5. Agility and Flexibility ()
Statement Agility and flexibility enable adaptation to changing business needs and emerging technologies Rationale • Agility and flexibility promote the reduction of technical debt • Flexible information flows ensure freedom and control while minimizing vendor lock-in • Modular, scalable, iterative and tiered designs enable adapting quickly to business needs • Loosely coupled components enable technology maintenance and innovation adoption while simplifying operational management Implications • Organizational change to promote agile methodologies with incremental delivery, and fostering a culture of continuous improvement • Excessive loosely coupled modularity can introduce complexity and cost • Environments with legacy technology requirements will have inherent challenges with vendor lock-in and technical debt. This must be understood and managed. • Implementing Agile methodology requires certification and skills
